Aubergine Agebitashi

Description

A dish from my fellow divines from the east, highlighting the most delectable aubergine!

Loki has a particular fondness for its bold yet subtle flavours.

Ingredients

  • Nasu:
    • 2 Japanese aubergines
    • neutral oil
    • 7.5cm daikon radish
    • 1 scallion
    • 1 knob ginger
    • pinch katsuobushi
  • Sauce:
    • 180ml dashi
    • 3Tbsp mirin
    • 3Tbsp sake
    • 3Tbsp soy sauce
    • 15g sugar

Steps

  1. Mix all sauce ingredients in a saucepan, bring to a boil then turn of the heat.
  2. Heat neutral oil in a pot for deep-frying.
  3. Halve aubergine and score them diagonally, then cut into 3 sections.
  4. Once oil is hot, gently fry aubergine for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Place fried aubergine in a single layer in a deep dish, then pour sauce over it and chill in fridge.
  6. Finely grate daikon and ginger, and thinly slice scallion.
  7. Serve each piece of aubergine sprinkled with some katsuobushi, topped with daikon, ginger and scallion, then lightly pour some sauce over it all.
