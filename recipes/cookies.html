Cookies

Description

Jormungandr's favourite cookies, when he's not too busy destroying the world.

(But secretly I got them from Cupcake Jemma's Youtube channel! Once again, perks of godhood.)

Ingredients

  • 190g cold butter
  • 135g caster sugar
  • 135g light brown sugar
  • 300g chocolate
  • 415g flour
  • 10g baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 2 eggs

Steps

  1. Roughly mix cubed butter and sugars together, leaving small nuggets of butter.
  2. Mix in chopped chocolate chips.
  3. Add remaining dry ingredients, and mix just until it becoming like a rough sand.
  4. Finally, mix in the eggs only long enough for the dough to come together.
  5. Divide into 12 balls and freeze! They'll keep for up to 3 months.
  6. When you're ready to bake, preheat the oven to 180 degrees with the paper-lined tray in.
  7. Place cookie balls in the oven for 12-14 mins, until the outside is golden brown.
  8. Leave to cool for 20 minutes! They will fall apart otherwise.
